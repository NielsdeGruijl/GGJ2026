===== implemented =====

- [[Homing missile]]
	- Spawns homing missiles that target closest enemy
- [[Bullet]]
	- Shoots bullet towards player cursor
- [[Lifesteal]]
	- Shoots life-stealing beam at random enemies
- [[Damaging masks]]
	- Makes masks deal damage per stack
- [[Trailblazer]]
	- Throws projectile that leaves a trail of explosions
- [[Lingering mask]]
	- Creates lingering pools that damage enemies
- [[Bouncing]]
	- Attack that bounces between enemies
